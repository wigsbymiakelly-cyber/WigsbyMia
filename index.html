My Story

Wigs by Mia Kelly

Affordable Luxury. Unapologetic Confidence.

My name is Mia Kelly, and I've been a professional stylist since 2009. I created Wigs by Mia Kelly for women who want effortless glam, confidence, and salon quality hair without compromise. Every wig is professionally colored, carefully handled, and personally inspected by me to ensure the highest level of quality and luxury.

My caps are designed for a comfortable fit, and every wig is completely glueless and beginner friendly, making it easy to achieve a flawless, salon quality look with confidence. All wigs are made with Brazilian Remy hair and should be treated with care just like natural hair. My standard wig style is 26 inches with 200% density for a full, luxurious look.

My goal is to give every woman beautiful, confidence boosting hair that feels as amazing as it looks.

My Mission

To provide high-quality luxury wigs at an affordable price while helping women feel confident, beautiful, and empowered every day.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about your Wigs by Mia Kelly purchase, care, and services.

Your Wigs by Mia Kelly box is specially designed to protect and safely store your wig, helping preserve its quality, shape, and longevity. We recommend storing it inside your box or on a wig stand when not being worn.

Category 01

Wig Care

With proper care and maintenance, your wig can last 1–2 years or longer. Longevity depends on how often it is worn and how well it is maintained. All wigs are made with Brazilian Remy hair.
To keep your wig looking its best:
  • Use sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid excessive heat styling
  • Detangle gently with a wide-tooth comb or wig brush
  • Store on a wig stand or in your Mia Kelly wig box when not in use
  • Schedule professional maintenance when needed
Yes. We recommend washing your wig every 4–6 weeks if worn regularly. Use lukewarm water with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and allow the wig to air dry completely.

Category 02

Install Preparation

To ensure the best results:
  • Hair must be clean and completely dry
  • Arrive with no oils, grease, or heavy products in your hair
  • Hair should be properly braided down unless a braid service is included
  • Bring your wig fully ready if not purchasing from Mia Kelly
Failure to follow preparation guidelines may result in additional service fees or appointment cancellation. Please arrive prepared to ensure the best possible results.

Category 03

Shipping & Processing

Wigs currently in stock typically ship within 3–5 business days after purchase. Tracking information will be sent once your order has shipped.
Pre-order wigs require 4–6 weeks for processing, depending on the level of customization. You will receive tracking information once your order has shipped.
Yes. Photos and videos will be sent prior to shipping to ensure customer satisfaction.

Category 04

Refund & Exchange Policy

Due to the nature of hair products, all wig sales are final. We do not offer refunds or exchanges once a wig has been purchased or shipped. Each wig is fully inspected by Mia Kelly before shipment to ensure the highest standard of quality.
